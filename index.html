Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

Last Updated: June 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Bank Balance Calculator app ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

1. Information We Collect

SMS Data: With your explicit permission, the app reads SMS messages from your device to extract bank transaction information and calculate your account balance. This data is processed locally on your device and not sent to any server.

Device Information: We may collect device-related data like model, OS version, and crash logs to improve app performance. This data does not personally identify you.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate your account balance from bank SMS messages.
  • To display recent transactions within the app (stored only on your device).
  • To improve the app and fix crashes or bugs (device logs only).

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permission:

  • Read SMS: To access bank-related SMS messages and calculate your balance. You must grant this permission for the app to function.

4. Data Storage & Security

All balance calculations and SMS processing happen locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, ads, or external SDKs that access your data.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
